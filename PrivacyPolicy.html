Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

At SafeHer, your privacy and security are our top priorities. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the SafeHer mobile application ("App").

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, and email address for account creation and identification.
  • Location Data: Real-time tracking is enabled only when safety features are active and stops upon deactivation.
  • Emergency Contacts: Accessed for functionality but not stored permanently.
  • Device Information: Such as phone model and OS version for security and troubleshooting.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To trigger emergency alerts and provide safety navigation.
  • To capture/send evidence to emergency contacts or law enforcement.
  • To process subscription payments securely.
  • To maintain and enhance app functionality and security.

4. Data Protection and Compliance

  • We use encryption and secure cloud storage for data safety.
  • Compliant with GDPR and India's Information Technology Act.
  • We do not share your personal data for marketing or ads.

5. Data Retention and User Control

  • Data is retained only as long as needed for services.
  • Users can disable location tracking anytime.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase for secure backend operations.
  • Google Maps for navigation and real-time location tracking.
  • Secure payment gateway for subscriptions.
  • Each third-party service has its own privacy policy.

7. Your Rights

  • Request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data.
  • Opt out by discontinuing app usage at any time.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy. Users will be notified of any significant changes via the App or email.
